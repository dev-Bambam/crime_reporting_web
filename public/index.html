Stand Against Crime

The Federal Polytechnic Ilaro Student Crime Report Center is your platform for reporting incidents. It is managed by the DSA (Directorate of Student Affairs) to ensure a safer environment for all students.

If you have witnessed or been a victim of any crime, please file a report. Your information is crucial to ensuring justice and maintaining a secure campus.

Stay informed about recent crime reports, and take proactive steps to protect yourself and your fellow students.

Let's work together to keep our campus safe. Report crimes and help us create a more secure environment for everyone.

Safeguarding Our Campus: The Role of the Student Crime Report Center (SCRC)

Together, We Can Make a Difference

In our connected campus life, security is paramount. From staying in touch with peers to accessing online resources, every student relies on a secure environment. Criminal activities, whether on campus or in the digital realm, pose a threat to us all. Our only defense is unity. By being vigilant and proactive, each member of the Polytechnic community can help protect themselves and others, ensuring that our campus remains safe for everyone.

Your Report Can Help Keep Our Campus Safe.

Your Input to the SCRC is Crucial.

By reporting incidents, you contribute to the overall safety of our Polytechnic community. The information you provide helps the DSA (Directorate of Student Affairs) investigate, monitor trends, and, when possible, take immediate action to prevent further harm. Your report not only protects you but also strengthens the security measures for the entire student body.
